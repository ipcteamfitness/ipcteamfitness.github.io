Overview

Come join our team of fly fitness women! In our Pole, Ribbon, Lyra, and Sexy Chair/Burlesque classes, all you have to do is move to your favorite music. Don't be intimidated - everyone in the class was a beginner at some point and classes have been designed for all fitness levels - no experience is required. At Aerial Athletes, our talented instructors will teach you all of the moves you need to know to help you not only tone up and get fit, but also to improve your performance skills. So, come on in, check us out, commit to the team, and you'll be a pro in no time. Not only does our studio offer some of the best fitness and performance classes available, but we also host bachelorette and birthday parties.
